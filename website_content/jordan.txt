Discover Excellence at Jordan High School

Welcome to Jordan High School, a beacon of academic excellence and innovation located in the vibrant community of Katy, Texas. Situated at 27500 Fulshear Bend Dr, our school is proud to be a distinguished member of the Katy Independent School District, renowned for its commitment to providing top-tier education.

At Jordan High School, we are home to a dedicated team of 117 professional educators, each boasting an average of 10 years of experience in the field of teaching. Our faculty’s expertise and passion for teaching are reflected in their longstanding tenure at our school, averaging 5 years. This stability and dedication ensure a nurturing and effective learning environment tailored to meet the needs of each student.

With a current enrollment of 1,933 students, Jordan High School offers a personalized learning experience with a favorable student-to-teacher ratio of 16.50. This setting not only facilitates more individualized attention and guidance but also fosters an intimate classroom atmosphere that enhances collaborative learning and personal growth.

In addition to our qualified teachers, we have 10 educational aids who provide additional support, ensuring that all students have the resources and assistance they need to succeed. Our comprehensive academic programs, coupled with cutting-edge facilities, prepare our students not just for college but for lifelong success.

Our campus buzzes with a wide array of extracurricular activities ranging from arts and athletics to science and social service clubs. At Jordan High School, we believe in educating the whole child and provide numerous opportunities for students to explore their interests and talents beyond the classroom.

Dual-Credit Programs in Katy ISD:
KISD partners with Houston Community College (HCC) to offer **CTE Dual-Credit courses** that allow students to earn both high-school and college credit in career-focused pathways ([Community Impact][9], [Katy Independent School District][11])
Notable programs include:
• Young Educators Program – associates of arts in teaching (AAT) with observation/student teaching in KISD; guaranteed admission to UH-Victoria Katy campus
• Medical Assisting – earn Level 1 Certificate in Medical Scribe (25 college credits)
• Real Estate – earn Level 1 Certificate in Residential Real Estate (16 college credits)
Dual-credit offerings extend to multiple subject areas via CTE and HCC articulation; students may graduate with college certificates or hours depending on program ([Katy Independent School District][11])

For the 2023–24 academic year:
• Over **2,000 students** enrolled district-wide in dual-credit courses
• They earned more than **16,000 college credit hours** ([Community Impact][9])
